WHY THIS EXISTS
KidRising was built by a kid with Tourette syndrome.
He knows what it feels like when the world wasn't built for you.
So he built it the other way around.
Every feature here was designed for the kids who need it most first.

Voice Control
TALK TO KIDRISING. IT LISTENS.
🎙️ HOW VOICE CONTROL WORKS
Press the button below and speak a command. KidRising will do it for you — no hands, no typing, no reading needed.
"go home" "open network" "open parliament" "open nation" "open fluffland" "open hyperblast" "open kidos" "read the screen" "high contrast" "large text" "calm mode" "stop reading"
WHY VOICE CONTROL?
Some kids can't use a mouse or keyboard. Some kids have limited movement. Some kids just find it easier to talk. KidRising works however you need it to work.
